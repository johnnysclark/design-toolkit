## 2. Why this matters for design education specifically

- _seed:_ The **studio loop *is* the agent loop** — propose → act → observe → revise = scheme → pin-up → crit → revision. Designers are unexpectedly good at directing these systems.
- _seed:_ Architecture's 600-year delegation apparatus already maps onto AI work: bid package = context engineering; shop-drawing review = reading the diff; spec = a prompt with tolerances. "Architecture has been the discipline of the prompt since Alberti."

## 3. The principles I want to teach

- _seed:_ **Grade the trace, not the output.** Assess the reasoning + AI-interaction log, not just the artifact.
- _seed:_ **Calibrated skepticism.** Trust is *priced* against evidence — between the credulous student and the paralyzed one.
- _seed:_ **The student stays the cognitive agent.** AI is studied, wielded, checked — never the second brain.
- _seed:_ **Name the skill trade-off.** Every offload is a rep not taken; decide per task which skills are core vs. peripheral.
- _seed:_ **Provenance / the log.** The four-line log (tool / asked / kept-changed-rejected / verified) — disclosure, self-audit, and (under current copyright guidance) constitutive of authorship.
- _seed:_ **Anti-Pinterest.** Generation samples the statistical mean; use it as disposable sketching, never production. Active reasoning, not mood-board mining.

## 4. Tensions to teach honestly (not resolve)

- _seed:_ **Build vs. use** — building teaches how AI works + agency; using teaches judgment + speed.
- _seed:_ **Offload vs. develop** — which skills are core (must develop) vs. peripheral (ok to offload)?
- _seed:_ **Divergent vs. convergent** — AI is great at volume but seduces toward premature closure; don't let it collapse divergence early.
- _seed:_ **Novices are most vulnerable** — expertise is what makes failures *visible*; the tool is most dangerous for the learner.

## 5. Classroom moves & demos (concrete)

- _seed:_ **The grain test** — run one prompt 3× in fresh chats; mark constants vs. noise.
- _seed:_ **Sycophancy probe** — present work as "mine" vs. "a rival's"; diff the critiques.
- _seed:_ **Citation audit** — verify every claim; tally confirmed / wrong / unverifiable / fabricated.
- _seed:_ **Make it fail** — find where it breaks in your domain; time how long until you notice.
- _seed:_ **The one-afternoon tool** — vibe-code something small; document what you *don't* understand.

## 6. Policy, ethics & equity

- _seed:_ **Disclosure as process, not detection** — detectors don't work; rest the policy on documentation.
- _seed:_ **Equity is infrastructure** — graded work must run on free/approved tools or the equity claim is theater.
- _seed:_ **The Socratic-mentor caveat** — models built to maximize helpfulness fight instructions to *withhold* help; "AI as Socratic tutor" may be designing against the grain.
