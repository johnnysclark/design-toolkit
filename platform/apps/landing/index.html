John Clark

A Midwest-based designer and educator, and Teaching Assistant Professor in the School of Architecture at the University of Illinois Urbana-Champaign, where he co-coordinates the first- and second-year design studios.

His work spans spatial design, object design, fabrication, planning, computation, and photography. His research asks how tools and workflows shape who can participate in design — with an emphasis on hybrid digital/analog practice and accessible methods, including AI-assisted visualization and low-vision technologies.
